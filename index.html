Research Projects Misc CV

I'm currently a Junior Research Scientist at New York University, working on climate finance at the Volatility and Risk Institute supervised by Johannes Stroebel and Theresa Kuchler.

I received my B.A. in Economics and Mathematics from McGill University, in Montreal, Canada.

I am particularly interested in game theory and the effect of incentive structures in an uncertain information environment. I am currently working on research relating to optimal climate policy and systemic risk in the banking system.
