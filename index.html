Research Projects Misc CV

I'm currently a Junior Research Scientist at New York University, working on climate finance at the Volatility and Risk Institute supervised by Johannes Stroebel and Theresa Kuchler.

I received my B.A. in Economics and Mathematics from McGill University, in Montreal, Canada.

My research interests are in network and spatial methods applied to environmental economics, climate and household finance, and international trade. I am currently working on projects relating to optimal climate policy and systemic risk in the banking system.

In my free time, I enjoy playing percussion instruments, and exploring NYC's amazing food scene, biking to new places, and hiking!
